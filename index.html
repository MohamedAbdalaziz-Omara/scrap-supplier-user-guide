Step-by-Step Activation Process

1 Account Creation by Administrator

The system administrator creates your supplier account in the system.

2 Receive Activation Information

You will receive an email at your registered email address containing your account activation information. Check your inbox and spam folder.

3 Enter Activation Information

Go to the activation page and enter the information you received in the email to start the activation process.

4 Receive OTP via Email

After entering the correct information, an OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your registered email address.

5 Enter OTP and Set Password

Enter the OTP you received in your email. If the OTP is correct, you will be prompted to set your password. Create a secure password following the system requirements.

6 Account Activated

Once your password is set, your account is activated and you can proceed to login.

Getting Started ‚Äì Account Onboarding & Access

After Account Activation

Once your account is activated (see Account Activation Process above), you can proceed with your first login.

First-Time Login

  • Navigate to the Supplier Portal login page.
  • Enter your registered username and the password you set during activation.
  • Click "Login" to access the portal.
  • Upon successful login, you will be redirected to the home screen.

Login & Security Management

Regular Login

  • Access the portal using your registered username and password.
  • Ensure you're using the correct credentials associated with your activated account.

Forgot Password

  • Click the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
  • Enter your registered email address.
  • An OTP (One-Time Password) will be sent to your email address.
  • Enter the OTP you received in your email.
  • If the OTP is correct, you will be prompted to enter a new password.
  • Create a new secure password following the system requirements.

Change Password

  • After logging in, navigate to your profile settings.
  • Select "Change Password" option.
  • Enter your current password and your new password.
  • Confirm the change to update your password.
